What is NAD+ and Why Does it Matter?

NAD+ stands for Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide. It is a compound found in every cell of your body and plays a critical role in energy production, DNA repair, and cellular health. The problem is that NAD+ levels naturally decline as we age — leading to fatigue, slower recovery, and reduced mental clarity.

Supplementing with ingredients that support NAD+ production is one of the fastest growing areas in health and wellness globally — and EnduNAD is designed specifically for this purpose.
