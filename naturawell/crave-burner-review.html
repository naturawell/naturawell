Why Is Appetite Control So Hard?

Understanding why cravings happen helps explain why Crave Burner works. Most people struggle with appetite because of:

  • Blood sugar spikes and crashes — unstable blood glucose causes intense hunger and sugar cravings throughout the day

  • Poor digestive comfort — bloating and digestive issues make it harder to maintain healthy eating habits consistently
