What Causes Acne?

Understanding why acne happens helps explain why Nonacne works. Acne is caused by a combination of factors:

  • Excess sebum — overactive oil glands clog pores and create the environment for acne to develop
  • Bacterial growth — bacteria thrive in clogged pores and cause inflammation and breakouts
  • Hormonal imbalance — hormonal fluctuations trigger increased sebum production especially during puberty, menstruation, and stress
  • Inflammation — the body's immune response to bacteria causes the redness and swelling associated with acne
  • Poor skin cell turnover — dead skin cells that are not shed properly block pores and worsen acne
