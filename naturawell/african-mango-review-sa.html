What is African Mango?

African Mango is a natural weight loss supplement derived from the extract of Irvingia Gabonensis — a fruit native to West and Central Africa. The seed extract has been studied for its ability to support healthy weight management, reduce appetite, and improve metabolic function.

Its slimming properties have been shown to surpass those of popular alternatives like acai berry extract, making it one of the most talked about natural weight loss discoveries in recent years. After gaining massive popularity in the United States it is now widely available in Europe and South Africa.
